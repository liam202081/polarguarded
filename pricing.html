Common questions
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time from your Stripe billing portal. No penalties, no questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay via Stripe.
Is my data stored when I scan a file?
Files are sent directly to VirusTotal for analysis. PolarGuarded does not store your files or scan results on our servers.
When is the VPN available?
The PolarGuarded VPN is currently in development and will be included in the Pro plan at no extra cost when it launches.
Is PolarGuarded based in Canada?
Yes. PolarGuarded is a Canadian business and your data is stored on Canadian servers.
